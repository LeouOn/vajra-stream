八十八佛大懺悔文
The Great Repentance of the 88 Buddhas

Intention: 愿一切众生离苦得乐，早证菩提。
May all beings be free from suffering and attain enlightenment.

I. OPENING ASPIRATION
大慈大悲愍众生 大喜大舍济含识
