### Orgonite Construction

Orgonite is a matrix that accumulates and transforms orgone energy (life force, prana, chi).

#### What is Orgonite?

**Composition:**
- 50% organic material (resin: polyester, epoxy, or resin casting compound)
- 50% inorganic material (metal shavings: aluminum, copper, brass, steel)
- Quartz crystal (whole or chips) to organize the energy field

**How it Works:**
1. **Accumulation:** Organic resin attracts and holds orgone
2. **Reflection:** Metal particles reflect orgone rapidly
3. **Transformation:** Continuous contraction/expansion creates "scrubbing" effect
4. **Organization:** Quartz crystal structures the field into coherent patterns

Orgonite continuously transforms DOR (dead/deadly orgone) into OR (organized, beneficial orgone).

#### DIY Orgonite Recipe

**Materials for 3-inch pyramid or disc:**
- 1 cup clear casting resin
- 1 cup metal shavings (aluminum, copper mix ideal)
- 1 small quartz point or handful of chips
- Mold (silicone pyramid, muffin tin, PVC pipe section)
- Copper wire (optional: spiral/coil design)

**Construction:**
1. Create copper spiral or Rodin coil (optional)
2. Place quartz crystal in mold pointing upward
3. Add copper coil around/beneath crystal
4. Mix metal shavings into container
5. Mix resin according to manufacturer directions
6. Pour resin over metal shavings in layers
7. Ensure crystal remains pointed upward
8. Allow to cure fully (24-72 hours)
9. Remove from mold, polish if desired

**Advanced Techniques:**
- **Layering:** Alternate resin and metal for "cake" effect
- **Rodin coils:** Wind copper in 1-2-4-8-7-5 pattern
- **Sacred geometry:** Embed copper wire in Flower of Life, Sri Yantra patterns
- **Crystal matrix:** Multiple small quartz points throughout
- **Gold/silver flakes:** Add precious metals for enhanced conductivity

**Cost:** $10-30 per piece DIY, $20-100 purchased

## Troubleshooting

### "Crystals don't seem to be vibrating"

**Check:**
- Bass shaker connected to correct audio output?
- Volume level 30-50% (too low won't transmit)?
- Bass shaker placed correctly under grid base?
- Try test: `python scripts/test_prayer_bowl_audio.py`

**Solution:**
- Increase volume gradually until subtle vibration felt
- Place hand on bass shaker to verify it's working
- Some vibration is subtle - not all crystals will visibly shake

### "GV measurements always low"

**Check:**
- Are you taking multiple measurements (`--count 20+`)?
- Is moon in waning phase or void of course (naturally lower energy)?
- Are crystals cleansed and charged recently?
- Have you performed personal attunement?

**Solution:**
- Cleanse and charge crystals (moonlight)
- Perform full attunement meditation
- Check timing - avoid unfavorable astrological periods
- Increase sample size - GV uses randomness, larger samples more accurate

### "Device feels 'dead' or unresponsive"

**Check:**
- When was last cleansing/charging?
- When was last personal attunement?
- Are there heavy EMF sources nearby (WiFi router, cell tower)?

**Solution:**
- Complete grid disassembly and cleansing
- Full moon charging overnight
- 30-minute attunement meditation with mudras
- Add shungite EMF protection around device
- Relocate away from EMF sources if possible

### "Getting opposite results (things getting worse)"

**Check:**
- Is intention phrased positively? (Use "health" not "remove disease")
- Are you attached to specific outcomes?
- Is there ethical alignment with highest good?
- Might this be temporary "healing crisis" (worse before better)?

**Solution:**
- Rephrase intentions in positive terms
- Add clause: "for highest good of all concerned"
- Release attachment to outcomes
- Trust the process - sometimes healing involves surfacing issues
- Reduce intensity/duration and build gradually
- Consult spiritual teacher or counselor

### "Audio plays but visualization doesn't generate"

**Check:**
- Is Pillow library installed? `pip install Pillow`
- Is `generated/rothko/` directory present?
- Check error messages in console

**Solution:**
```bash
mkdir -p generated/rothko
pip install Pillow
python scripts/radionics_operation.py --intention "test" --duration 60
```

### "Software can't connect to LLM"

**Check:**
- Environment variables set: `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY` or `OPENAI_API_KEY`?
- OR local model in `./models/` directory?

**Solution:**
- Use `--no-prayer` flag to skip LLM-generated prayers
- System will use traditional prayers from library
- Or set up API key or local model per QUICKSTART.md
