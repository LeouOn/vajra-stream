3. **Alternate** these two commands 10-20 times

### Problem: Never get floating needle

**Possible causes:**
1. Sensitivity too low ‚Üí Increase to 1.5-2.0
2. Session too short ‚Üí Continue 30+ minutes
3. Connection not established ‚Üí Return to Phase 2 spotting

### Problem: Constant rockslams

**Possible causes:**
1. Very heavy charge on population (actually good sign - real contact)
2. Need more powerful support:
   - Increase precious metal amount
   - Add second practitioner
   - Use stronger prayers/mantras
   - Extend session duration

1. Electromagnetic interference (computers, phones, wifi)

**Solutions:**
- Move to quieter space
- Turn off nearby electronics
- Practice shamatha to stabilize mind first
- Lower sensitivity temporarily

### Problem: High coherence but no floating needle

**Meaning**: Good connection and focus, but charge not yet released

**Action**:
- Continue session - this is working!
